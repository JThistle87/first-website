The Bench Squad

These players may not always be on the court, but they’re the backbone of the team during practice and beyond.

  • Sebastiano Sani:

    • Sani is a 6'8 sophmore outside hitter from Maryland USA. As a sophmore Sani has not seen much play time yet but still has a big impact on the team consintly being a serving sub in big time games and facing lots of pressure. Sani has a brightfuture as both the starting players at his postion are planning to graduate next year which could possibly lead Sani to a starting spot for the beach next year!

  • Nato Dickinson:

    • The Energy Booster of the team. Nato is a redshirt senoir with the beach who has gone through many postion changes throughout the four years he has spent with the beach. Over the past few years Nato has been the main hype person on the bench leading almost every cheer that the bench does for every point. Even tho Nato has not played much time with the Beach the team would not be the same without him around.
